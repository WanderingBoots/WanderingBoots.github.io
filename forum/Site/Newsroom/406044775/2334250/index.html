It's that time again. He's about to escape, and I hope we're all prepared. It's the fourth annual Starmen.Net WHAMAGEDDON!

If this is your first time, please take a moment to review the rules. If this is your second, third, or fourth time, please review the rules anyway, you don't want to be the person who eliminates themselves on a weird technicality.

This game is based on the honor system! If you spend December 8th with the song on loop for some reason, and then roll up on December 25th to declare that you've won, then the only person you've cheated is yourself! If you've decided to play, then reveal yourself as soon as possible! It's not fun to declare victory if no one even knew you were playing! I'll even put you on a list of currently active players that I will probably remember to update sometimes! Here's a quick rundown of the rules for anyone who couldn't be bothered to click through to the WHAMAGEDDON website... Rule 1: The objective is to go as long as possible without hearing WHAM's Christmas classic; "Last Christmas". Rule 2: The game starts on December 1st, and ends at midnight on December 24th. Use your local timezone, if you'd like. Rule 3: Only the original version applies. Enjoy the ?#!$&%! out of remixes and covers. Rule 4: You're out as soon as you recognize the song. Bonus Rule: Post here when you get eliminated! It's so much more fun watching your competition drop like flies throughout the month! PvP: While we can't stop you from deliberately sending your friends to Whamhalla, the intention is that this is a survival game. Not a Battle Royale. So play nice!
